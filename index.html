Super Smash Bros

Super Smash Bros is a series of 2.5d fighting games consisting of Nintendo characters and guest characters from other games. Though you can play it in a similar fashion as other fighting games it differs in many ways. While in traditional fighting games your objective is to reduce your opponents health to 0, in Super Smash Bros your goal is to increase your opponents percentage which affects how far they are sent flying when they are hit by an attack and try to send them past the stage boundry resulting in a kill. Players use combinations of attacks to reduce their opponents lives, or stock to zero resulting in a win.
